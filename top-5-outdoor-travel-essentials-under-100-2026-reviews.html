1. ENO DoubleNest Camping Hammock

A legendary lightweight hammock that has earned its reputation among campers and backpackers for good reason.

If you've spent any time in the outdoor community, you've almost certainly heard of ENO. The DoubleNest hammock is one of their signature products, and after years of refinement, it remains one of the most trusted options in its class. It packs down into its own internal stuff sack to roughly the size of a softball, yet comfortably supports two people — which is impressive for something so compact. The nylon fabric is breathable and strong, and setup between two trees takes under two minutes once you have a basic system down. Our main criticism is that the required suspension straps are sold separately, which feels a bit like buying a bicycle without pedals. Still, for the price, the hammock itself is exceptional quality.

  • Durable, breathable nylon construction
  • Comfortable for one or two people

  • Suspension straps sold separately
  • No built-in bug net
  • Can be warm in hot, humid weather
