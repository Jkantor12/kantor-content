Weighted blankets have moved well beyond trend status and are now a staple recommendation from sleep specialists for people of all ages. The result is a hugging sensation that many users, including ourselves, find remarkably calming during both sleep and rest. The breathable cotton outer layer helps manage warmth, though hot sleepers may still find it too toasty in summer months.
